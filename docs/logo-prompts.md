# Racc Logo Generation Prompts (for nanobanana)

## Prompt 1: 字母尾巴融合（最推荐）

> A minimalist logo design for a software called "racc". The last letter "c" extends into a long, fluffy striped raccoon tail curving upward elegantly. The text uses a clean modern sans-serif font in dark charcoal gray. The raccoon tail has classic gray and black stripe pattern. White background, flat vector style, app icon quality, high contrast, simple and memorable.

**思路：** 模仿你提到的那个游戏风格，让 "racc" 的最后一个 c 自然延伸出浣熊尾巴，简洁又有辨识度。

---

## Prompt 2: 浣熊面具 + 文字

> A minimalist logo for "racc", where the two "c" letters are stylized to resemble a raccoon's eye mask — the negative space between them forms the dark mask pattern around two small bright eyes. Clean geometric design, modern sans-serif typography, dark gray and black color palette with a subtle teal accent. Flat vector, white background, suitable for app icon and GitHub repository.

**思路：** 利用两个 c 的形状暗示浣熊标志性的眼罩，更抽象、更 tech 感。

---

## Prompt 3: 浣熊吉祥物 + 文字（分离式）

> A cute minimalist raccoon mascot sitting next to the word "racc" in lowercase. The raccoon is in a simple geometric style with rounded shapes, dark gray body, black eye mask, and a striped tail wrapped around itself. The raccoon looks clever and friendly, slightly tilting its head. The text "racc" is in a clean modern font. Flat vector illustration, limited color palette (gray, black, white, teal accent), white background.

**思路：** 不做融合，浣熊作为独立吉祥物出现在文字旁边，适合做 favicon 和社交媒体头像。

---

## Prompt 4: 字母 R 变浣熊

> A logo where the letter "R" is stylized as a raccoon face — the vertical stroke forms the head, the bump forms a round ear, and the diagonal leg becomes a striped raccoon tail. Next to it, "acc" in clean lowercase sans-serif. Minimal flat vector design, dark charcoal and black with subtle gray stripes on the tail. White background, modern tech aesthetic.

**思路：** 只把首字母 R 变成浣熊脸，其余字母保持干净，平衡了趣味性和专业感。

---

## Prompt 5: 浣熊爪印 + 终端光标

> A minimalist logo combining a raccoon paw print with a terminal cursor blinking symbol. The word "racc" in monospace font below. The paw print subtly incorporates a ">" terminal prompt symbol. Dark gray, black, and teal green color scheme. Flat vector, clean modern design, white background, developer tool aesthetic.

**思路：** 突出 Racc 作为终端 IDE 工具的身份，浣熊爪 + 终端光标的结合，更偏 developer tool 调性。

---

## 使用建议

- **首选 Prompt 1 或 4** — 文字与浣熊融合最自然，辨识度高
- **Prompt 3** 作为备选 — 如果融合效果不理想，独立吉祥物更容易生成高质量结果
- 生成后可能需要用 Figma/Illustrator 微调，AI 生成的 Logo 文字部分往往需要手动修正
- 建议同时生成 **方形版**（app icon）和 **横向版**（README header）
